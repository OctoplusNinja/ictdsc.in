Paper Submission


All authors must comply with guidelines while preparing their manuscripts. The images and tables must be self-drawn or must be used with proper permissions and copyrights. All the equations used in the manuscript must be written using equation editor. The similarity index/plagiarism should not be more than 5% from a single source and must be less than 20% (including self-plagiarism).


Copyright Form


You will have to send us the signed copy of Copyright form as a scanned PDF, after the acceptance of your manuscript. The corresponding author should be available to check the paper before it is published. Please note that once a paper has been delivered to Springer, changes relating to the authorship of the paper cannot be made. Authors’ names cannot be added or deleted, their order cannot be changed, and the corresponding author cannot be altered.


Submission


The papers will be evaluated on their originality, structure, clarity and relevance. Other important instructions include the following:

*     Authors are required to submit Authentic & high Quality Substantial Research Manuscripts.

*     All Submission should have less than 20% Similarity.

*     Please submit your manuscript through the link provided above.

*     Submissions via e-mails will not be entertained.

*     Authors Are Required To Submit Paper Whose Size Should Be 10-20 Pages.

Authors should consult Springer’s authors’ guidelines and use their proceedings templates, either for LaTeX or for Word, for the preparation of their papers. Springer encourages authors to include their ORCIDs in their papers. In addition, the corresponding author of each paper, acting on behalf of all of the authors of that paper, must complete and sign a Consent-to-Publish form. The corresponding author signing the copyright form should match the corresponding author marked on the paper. Once the files have been sent to Springer, changes relating to the authorship of the papers cannot be made.

Publication


    Download your template package for Word or LaTex.

    Do not add page numbers or headers/footers.

    Do not alter the margins of our templates.

    Paper size is 8.5 x 11.

    The paper must include the author name and full affiliation address including country.

    Use clear, legible graphics and diagrams contained within the margins.

    Do not use copyrighted material without permission and proper crediting.

    All pages are to be in portrait.

    References are prepared using our sample reference guidelines (available in the template package). References can only be linked if they are complete.

    Embed all fonts into your manuscript PDF.

Presentation


Oral presentations should strictly comply with the content of the corresponding paper. The duration of the presentation will be determined before the event, authors will be informed with sufficient time. Speakers must be registered in the conference, just like any other attendee.


Proceedings
